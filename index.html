About Danny's Concrete

14 Years of
Craftsmanship &

Danny's Concrete is a family-owned business with 14 years of hands-on experience in high-quality concrete services across the Norwalk and greater Los Angeles area. Before starting this business, Danny worked with 3 different companies to build the skills and knowledge to do the job right.

We specialize in driveways, patios, sidewalks, stamped concrete, pool decks, steps, outdoor kitchens, fire pits, and concrete repair. Every job is approached with fair pricing, quality workmanship, and a commitment to getting it done right.
